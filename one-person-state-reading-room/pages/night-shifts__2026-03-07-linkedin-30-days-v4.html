V4 breaks the pattern:

  • running callbacks: wife appears 4 times. AI night agent returns. 3-node constraint threads through. "the embassy is online" bookends
  • anti-content: 3 posts that attack LinkedIn FROM LinkedIn
  • genuine weirdness: lean into what's actually bizarre about Ray's life — not package it into lessons

every post should make someone pause before hitting "like."

cars didn't just beat buses. cars rebuilt cities so you couldn't walk.

Ivan Illich called this "radical monopoly." when one type of product reshapes reality so alternatives become impossible.

now apply it to cognition.

job listings require "AI proficiency." publishers assume AI-edited prose. each one seems reasonable. together they rebuild the cognitive commons so that thinking without AI becomes a handicap.

MIT Media Lab, EEG study: 83% of AI users couldn't quote their own essay minutes after writing it. the neurons that fire when you struggle through writing go quiet when the AI does it for you.

the effort is the learning. remove the effort, remove the learning.

my wife asked me: "so your entire product is about making people not need your product?"

i didn't have an answer. she went to bed. i sat with it for an hour.

if you can't explain why someone should stop using your tool and still be fine, you haven't built a tool. you've built a dependency.

that silence after her question — that's the only honest metric i have.

ILLICH'S KEY WORKS (know these)

  • 4 tests of conviviality

  • Radical monopoly (not brand dominance — category dominance that eliminates alternatives)

Deschooling Society (1971)

  • Schools create the need for schooling (parallel: AI creates the need for AI)
  • "The pupil is 'schooled' to confuse teaching with learning" → users are 'prompted' to confuse generating with understanding
  • Learning webs > institutions — peer matching, skill exchanges

Medical Nemesis / Limits to Medicine (1975)

  • Iatrogenesis: clinical, social, cultural — you already mapped this to AI
  • The medical system creates the diseases it claims to cure
  • Parallel: AI creates the cognitive dependency it claims to solve

The Vernacular (concept across multiple works)

  • "Any value that was homebred, homemade, derived from the commons"
  • You already used this: self.md as vernacular configuration
  • Key tension Sajay cares about: can the vernacular survive digitization?

Shadow Work (1981)

  • Unpaid work that industrial society requires (commuting, housework, consuming)
  • AI parallel: the shadow work of prompt engineering, fine-tuning your tools, managing your AI stack
